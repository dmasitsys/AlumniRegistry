Welcome to the Per Scholas Alumni Registry website designed and developed by the Visionaries.
The Visionaries are 5 professionals and graduates of Per Scholas. They are Donovan Salmon, Demarco Siler, 
Elizabeth Teena Lalu, Emmanuel Ubhimhiye, and Babiyew Zewdie.


The website was created to curate a community of diverse IT Professionals. Membership in the community
is based on an application process. Each application is evaluated before a candidate's membership is approved 
by a panel of industry subject matter experts (SME). Select apply for membership for more information.

Visit the Developers page to learn more about the professionals, their vision, projects
and their mission to bring awareness to STARS-Skilled Through Alternative Routes.
